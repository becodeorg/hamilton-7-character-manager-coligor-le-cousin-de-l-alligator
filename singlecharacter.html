Character Manager

David Martinez

lives in the world of Cyberpunk: Edgerunners in the 2060s

David Martinez is the protagonist of Cyberpunk: Edgerunners. He was a mercenary and a member of the Edgerunner crew who was involved in a series of harrowing events that ultimately resulted in him attaining the status of a legend in Night City during the year 2076.

David Martinez is a Latino teenager with light brown eyes and brown hair that he wears in a quaffed hairstyle, with the sides shaved. He has a thin athletic build typical of a teenager his age. David's typical style is Kitsch, sporting a black t-shirt, two golden chain necklaces (one with a cross), gray cropped sweatpants with two yellow straps on either side, and white tennis shoes with blue straps. Later on, he starts to wear his mother's yellow EMT jacket with a blue luminescent collar on the inside of the material, with a green logo painted on the back.
